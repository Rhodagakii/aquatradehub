About Aqua Trade Hub

streamlining the sea food supply chain through innovative digital solutions

A warm welcome!

Aqua Trade Hub is a pioneering startup revolutionizing market access and logistics for fishermen and fish farmers across the globe. We empower our clients with innovative technology and personalized support to streamline their operations and unlock new growth opportunities.

vision

A connected, efficient, and sustainable seafood ecosystem that benefits all stakeholders.

mission

To empower fishermen, farmers, and buyers with the tools and data they need to thrive in the global seafood market.

values

Transparency, collaboration, innovation, and environmental stewardship.
